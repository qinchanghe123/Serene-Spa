FAQs

Before your visit.

A few helpful answers so your appointment feels simple, comfortable, and stress-free.

Please arrive about 10 minutes before your appointment so you have time to check in, settle in, and begin your session without feeling rushed.

If you have an injury, recent surgery, or medical concern, please consult your healthcare provider first and let us know before your session begins.

If you need to cancel or reschedule, please call as early as possible so we can adjust the appointment time and assist other guests.

Parking availability may vary by time of day around the location. Please allow a few extra minutes for parking before your scheduled appointment.
